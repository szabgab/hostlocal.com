Hostlocal Ltd. - staff
Gabor Szabo the owner of Hostlocal Ltd. has been working in the IT industry since 1993.

Gabor has already won a programming award in his high-school years. Since then he has earned his BSc. in Computer Sciences and his MBA in Information Technology both from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.

Since finishing the University Gabor has been working as programmer and system administrator till 1999 when he started his own company.
